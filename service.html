Our Classes:

1.Cardio:

Cardio exercise, also known as cardiovascular or aerobic exercise, is any activity that elevates your heart rate and breathing, making you breathe harder. It involves using large muscle groups in a rhythmic, repetitive manner. Examples include walking, running, swimming, and cycling. Cardio is a cornerstone of fitness, helping to improve cardiovascular health, burn calories, and manage weight.

Yoga is an ancient Indian practice that integrates the body, mind, and spirit through a variety of physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation.
